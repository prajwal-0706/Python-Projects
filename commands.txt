Linux command: 

1) rpm -q : (Package manager to check the package name)

2) netstat -tnlp (Active ports in system)

3) ls -a (to show all files even hidden)

4) df -h (Free disk checkup)

5) ifconfig (IP's of our PC's)

6) vlc ( To open the vlc media player)

7) baobab (To open the Disk Analyzer)

8) useradd (To add the new user)

9) passwd (To change the password of current user)
 
9) systemctl status (To check the software is on or not)

10) ps -aux (To check the active processes )

11) lsblk (To show the partitions)
